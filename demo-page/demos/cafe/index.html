Our Story

More Than Just Coffee

Founded in 2015, Savoria began as a small corner cafe with a simple mission: serve exceptional coffee and create a welcoming space for our community.

Today, we're proud to source our beans from ethical farms worldwide, roast them in-house, and serve you the freshest cup possible. Every pastry is baked fresh each morning by our talented team.
